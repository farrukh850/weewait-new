About us

WeeWait was built by parents, for parents — to make finding childcare less stressful and more human.

— Danielle, Founder of WeeWait
with her daughter Amelia

My Story as a Parent

When I was looking for daycare for my child, I was overwhelmed. Every center had its own form, its own process, its own waitlist — and no way to know where I stood. I spent hours filling out the same information over and over, making calls, following up… and still felt like I was in the dark.

I kept thinking:

“Why isn’t there one place where I can manage all of this — easily?”

That question led to WeeWait.

Now, instead of chaos, we offer clarity. Instead of chasing daycares, parents can focus on their kids. WeeWait helps families like mine (and maybe yours) simplify the daycare search — and take back their time and peace of mind.

I Got Off the Waitlist Without the Chaos

"As a mom of three, I've been through it all — endless forms, phone calls, and waiting weeks just to hear back. WeeWait was a total game changer. I created one profile, selected a few daycares, and the platform took care of the rest. I didn't have to track anything manually — I got a notification the moment a spot opened up. It honestly made my life so much easier."

— Emily, mom of a 4-year-old
